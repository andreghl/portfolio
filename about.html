About

I am an Economics Bachelor student at Maastricht University in the Netherlands. I developed an interest in Econometrics and Statistics and have, for this reason, included more Econometric courses in my curriculum. This website is a place to write about my interests and to further improve my knowledge in hopes of pursuing a Master's in Econometrics, Actuarial Sciences, or an other related field, and possibly a PhD.

This interest in Econometrics has led me to learn programming languages such as R and Julia. These languages have introduced me to statistical computing applied to economic and financial analysis. The third language is Tex which I, alongside Markdown, have mostly used for note-taking.
