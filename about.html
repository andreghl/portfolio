About

I am an Economics Bachelor student at Maastricht University in the Netherlands. I developed an interest in Econometrics and Statistics and have, for this reason, included more Econometric courses in my curriculum. This blog is a place to write about to interests and to further improve my knowledge in hopes of pursuing a Master's in Econometric, Actuarial Sciences, or a related field.

Activities

ArkeaTech - Bookkeeping
Sept 2021 - Mar 2022

Our project was to help people aged 60+ who were uncomfortable with new technologies to develop the skills needed to use their digital products on their own. Unfortenately, our project suffered due to the Covid-19 crisis. We decided to discontinue the project in early 2022 to mitigate the risk of contamination (since we were dealing with a population at risk). This project was started thanks to the Les Jeunes Entreprises initiative in Belgium.

Scope Maastricht - Database
Mar 2024 - Present

Scope is the study association of the school of Business and Economics of Maastricht University. The aim of the database committee is to create a single database containing all the known information about our members spread out in different spreadsheet files. For this project, I cleaned the different datasets with R or in Excel and formatted the data across the different files.
