About


I am a BSc Economics and Business Economics student at Maastricht University. I have developed an interest in econometrics and statistics during my studies and I have accumulated some skills in statistical programming. This blog is a place to write about my interests and to showcase my abilities.


Activities


ArkeaTech - Bookkeeping
Sept 2021 - Mar 2022

Our project was to help people aged 60+ who were uncomfortable with new technologies to develop the skills needed to use their digital products on their own. Unfortenately, our project suffered due to the Covid-19 crisis. We decided to discontinue the project in early 2022 to mitigate the risk of contamination (since we were dealing with a population at risk). This project was started thanks to the "Les Jeunes Entreprises" initiative in Belgium.


Scope Maastricht - Database
Mar 2024 - Present

Scope is the study association of the school of Business and Economics of Maastricht University. The aim of the database committee is to create a single database containing all the known information about our members spread out in different spreadsheet files. For this project, I cleaned the different datasets with R or in Excel and formatting the data across the different files.
